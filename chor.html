Quick rules

  • Visit any Safe Zone to collect that zone's unique sticker (once each).
  • Each Safe Zone holds max 10 chors at a time — if it's full, wait outside.
  • Outside Safe Zones, police can catch you any time.
  • Caught → lose a life, 2 minutes in jail, then back in from any Safe Zone.
  • 3 lifelines total — lose all 3 and you're eliminated.
  • Collect all 10 stickers to win!
