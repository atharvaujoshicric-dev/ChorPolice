Game rules — quick reminder

  • Visit a checkpost alone → safe ticket (no penalty, but doesn't count as progress).
  • Visit a checkpost with exactly 10 chors together → you get the stamp (counts toward winning).
  • Any other group size at a checkpost → police may catch you.
  • While travelling between checkposts, groups don't protect you — police can catch you alone.
  • 3 lifelines total. Lose all 3 and you're eliminated.
  • Stamp every checkpost to win!
